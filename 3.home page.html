A Moving Effort for a Hale Society.
Assured Best Care Hospital (ABC Hospital), is a chennai based 100 bed
hospital providing secondary and tertiary health care for the population since
2003. The hospital has won the trust of its clientele by its committed, round
-the-clock service and patient-centric approach.

The hospital is an established referral centre for Critical Care, Internal
Medicine, Traumatology, Urology, Pulmonology, Nephrology & Organ
Transplantation. The hospital strives to provide modern health care at
affordable costs and high safety standards.
